Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What are your pricing ranges? ‚ñº
Our 60-minute massage sessions range from $95-$120, and 90-minute sessions range from $140-$175, depending on the type of massage. We also offer package discounts for clients who book multiple sessions. Contact us for current pricing and special offers.
What should I expect during my first session? ‚ñº
You'll start with a brief consultation where your therapist will discuss your health history, areas of concern, and wellness goals. We'll explain the massage process and answer any questions. You'll then relax in our private treatment room while your therapist provides personalized care tailored to your needs.
How does the booking process work? ‚ñº
Simply fill out our booking form below with your contact information and preferred massage type. After you submit, our AI assistant will call you shortly to confirm your appointment, answer any questions, and help you schedule a time that works best for you.
What is your cancellation policy? ‚ñº
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. This allows us to offer your appointment time to other clients. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee. We understand that emergencies happen and will work with you on a case-by-case basis.
Do you offer same-day appointments? ‚ñº
We do our best to accommodate same-day requests based on therapist availability. Submit a booking request or call us directly at (415) 555-0148, and we'll check our schedule. For guaranteed availability, we recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance.
